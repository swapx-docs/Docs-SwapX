## ðŸš€ How to Trade

Trading on SwapX is designed to be straightforward. SwapX handles all the trade routing and calculations for you, allowing you to focus purely on the exchange.

***

### Trading Setup

Before you begin trading, ensure you have:

1. **A Wallet:** If you don't have one, learn how to get a crypto wallet here.
2. **Gas Fees:** You'll need some native chain token (e.g., XOC) to cover transaction (Gas) fees. Learn how to get Gas here.

### Trading on SwapX Exchange

Follow these steps to complete a token exchange:

1.  Go to the **Trade page**: [Click Here](https://swapx.exchange/).

    <div data-with-frame="true"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (26).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>
2.  Unlock your wallet by clicking **"Connect Wallet"** (or use the "Connect" button in the top right corner). If you haven't connected your wallet to SwapX yet, you can follow the guide here.

    <div data-with-frame="true"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (27).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>
3.  In the **"You pay" section (top)**, select the token you wish to sell from the drop-down menu. Ensure you have a sufficient balance, which is displayed above the token selection.

    <div data-with-frame="true"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (28).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>
4.  In the **"You receive" section (bottom)**, select the token you wish to buy from its drop-down menu.

    http://googleusercontent.com/image\_generation\_content/3
5.  Enter the amount you wish to exchange in **either** the top ("You pay") or bottom ("You receive") section. The amount in the other section will be automatically estimated.

    <div data-with-frame="true"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (29).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>
6.  Review all the details carefully. If this is the first time you are trading a particular token, you may first need to **"Enable \[Your Token Name]"** by clicking the button. Once enabled, click the **"Trade"** button.\


    <div data-with-frame="true"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (39).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>
7.  A confirmation window will appear with more details. Double-check all the information. When ready, click **"Confirm Exchange"**. Your connected wallet will then prompt you to confirm this action within its interface.

    <div data-with-frame="true"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (33).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>
8.  Congratulations! Your transaction is now complete. You can view its details on a blockchain explorer.
