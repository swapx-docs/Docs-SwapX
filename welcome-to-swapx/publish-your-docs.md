<details>

<summary>What is SwapX?</summary>

SwapX is a native decentralized exchange (DEX) built on the XONE public chain. It is based on the Uniswap v2/v3 protocol and supports token exchange, liquidity provision and fiat currency trading functions.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Which blockchain ecosystem does SwapX belong to?</summary>

SwapX is a DEX deployed on the XONE Chain, which is highly integrated with ecological modules such as XOC tokens, USDH stablecoins, and BVI behavioral incentive mechanisms.

</details>

<details>

<summary>ls SwapX a decentralized platform?</summary>

SwapX is completely decentralized. Users keep their own private keys and assets. All transactions are executed through on-chain smart contracts without trusting third parties.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Does SwapX host assets?</summary>

SwapX Without custody of assets, you always interact with smart contracts through your personal wallet. Asset security is controlled by your private key.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Which wallets does SwapX support?</summary>

SwapX supports MetaMask, TokenUp, Trust Wallet, OKX Wallet, Coinbase Wallet, Bitget, TokenPocket, Phantom, etc.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Does SwapXrequire real-name registration?</summary>

SwapX does not require account registration or real-name registration, and can be traded using a wallet

</details>

<details>

<summary>How to connect a wallet to SwapX?</summary>

Visit the SwapX official website, click Connect Wallet in the upper right corner ‚Üí Select a supported wallet ‚Üí Complete on-chain authorization.

</details>

<details>

<summary>How to exchange tokens in SwapX?</summary>

Enter swapx.exchange, click Exchange, select the token pair and enter the quantity, and confirm the transaction after authorization.

</details>

<details>

<summary>What assets does SwapX support?</summary>

SwapX currently supports XOC, WXOC, USDH, PANDE, PHX, T1, PANDA, PNUTX, TEST, NUTX, PEPE, etc., and more ecological tokens will be connected in the future.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Does SwapX support multiple chains?</summary>

SwapX currently supports the XONE mainnet, and ETH, BSC, and Tron support will be opened soon.

</details>

<details>

<summary>How much handling fee will SwapX charge for transactions?</summary>

The default handling fee of SwapX is 0.3%, all of which is used to reward liquidity providers. A 0.05% protocol fee may be introduced in the future.

</details>

<details>

<summary>What will you get from adding liquidity to SwapX?</summary>

You will get LP Token and share the transaction fee in proportion. Different trading pairs support different fee levels, the most common is 0.3%.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can SwapX liquidity be removed at any time?</summary>

Yes, users can click Remove Liquidity in Fund Pool ‚Üí My Holdings and confirm the wallet operation.

</details>

<details>

<summary>What is impermanent loss in SwapX?</summary>

When the price of the token you provide fluctuates greatly, you may lose some of your earnings compared to holding the token directly. It is recommended to choose a stablecoin portfolio with low volatility.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Will SwapX launch trading mining or incentive plans?</summary>

Yes. SwapX will regularly launch trading competitions, LP reward activities, holding subsidies and other operational plans.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Does SwapX have governance tokens or governance functions?</summary>

SwapX is currently being promoted by the XONE Foundation and the ecological community. In the future, a governance module (XOC/XGT) will be introduced for joint decision-making by the community.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Does SwapX collect user privacy information?</summary>

No, SwapX does not store user identity information or wallet private keys, and only retains necessary on-chain data interaction information for service optimization.

</details>

<summary>How to adjust the slippage setting of SwapX?</summary>

Click the setting button on the exchange interface to customize the slippage tolerance (such as 0.1%, 0.5%, 1%) to control transaction deviation.

</details>

<details>

<summary>What is the liquidity pool of SwapX?</summary>

The liquidity pool is a smart contract fund pool composed of token pairs provided by users, which is used to realize automatic market making and token exchange.

</details>

<details>

<summary>How to add liquidity to SwapX?</summary>

Go to the Fund Pool page, select the token pair, enter the quantity, and confirm the addition after authorization to obtain LP Token.

</details>

<details>

<summary>What are the benefits of adding liquidity to SwapX?</summary>

You can get a 0.3% commission share for each transaction, which is settled according to the liquidity ratio, and can also be used to participate in incentive activities.

</details>

<details>

<summary>How to remove liguidity from SwapX?</summary>

Go to the My Holdings page, select the pool and click Remove, set the ratio and confirm.

</details>

<details>

<summary>What is SwapX's LP Token?</summary>

It is the certificate of your liquidity provision, which can be used to redeem commission dividends or participate in incentive activities.

</details>
