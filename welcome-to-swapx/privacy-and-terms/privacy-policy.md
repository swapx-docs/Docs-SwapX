### 2. Information We Collect

We only collect the **minimum necessary information** to provide and optimize the Services.

#### 2.1 Public On-Chain Data

Your interactions with the decentralized Services generate data stored publicly on the **XONE Chain and other third-party blockchain networks**. This includes:

* **Wallet Address:** Identifies the initiator and recipient of your transactions.
* **Transaction Records:** Details like cross-chain exchange, token purchase, Swap transaction amounts, token types, and timestamps.
* **Smart Contract Interaction:** Data related to your interaction with SwapX smart contracts (e.g., exchange requests, gas fees).

> **Note:** On-chain data is inherently **public** and viewable by anyone via a blockchain browser. We do not control the publicity of this data.

#### 2.2 Limited Off-Chain Data

When you use the SwapX website (`https://swapx.exchange/`) or the Document Center (`https://docs.swapx.exchange/study`), we may collect the following limited information for **user experience optimization**:

* **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, browser version, and language settings.
* **Usage Data:** Access time, page browsing history, and click behavior.

#### 2.3 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use **cookies and similar technologies** (e.g., local storage) to analyze website traffic and improve user experience. You may disable cookies via your browser settings, but this may affect some functionality.

#### 2.4 Third-Party Payment Data

When purchasing tokens through a **third-party provider**, SwapX **does not directly collect or store** your payment information (e.g., bank card number). We may only receive the following associated data:

* **Payment Status:** Whether the transaction was successful.
* **Associated Wallet Address:** Used to send the purchased tokens to you.

#### 2.5 User-Provided Information

Information you willingly provide when seeking support or engaging with the community:

* **Support Requests:** Information provided when submitting questions via email (`support@swapx.exchange`) or Discord (e.g., wallet addresses).
* **Community Participation:** Information shared when participating in activities on Twitter, Telegram, or Discord.

### 3. How We Use Your Information

We strictly limit the use of collected information to the following purposes:

* **Provide Services:** Process cross-chain exchanges, token purchases, and Swap transactions, and display transaction status.
* **Improve Services:** Analyze usage data and optimize website performance and user experience.
* **Customer Support:** Respond to support requests and resolve technical issues.
* **Security & Compliance:** Screen wallet addresses via blockchain analysis tools to detect and prevent fraud or illegal activities, ensuring compliance with **AML/CTF** regulations.
* **Not for Marketing:** **SwapX does not use your information for marketing purposes.**

***

### 4. How We Share Your Information

SwapX will **not sell or rent** your personal information. We may share information under the following circumstances:

* **On-Chain Data:** Publicly stored on XONE Chain and related blockchains (Ethereum, TRON, Solana, Binance Chain) and accessible by anyone.
* **Blockchain Analysis Providers:** Wallet addresses may be shared with third-party providers to detect illegal activities. **No personally identifiable information is shared.**

### 5. Data Storage and Security

#### 5.1 Storage

* **On-Chain Data:** Stored on XONE Chain and related blockchains, maintained by the respective network.
* **Off-Chain Data:** Stored on SwapX servers, subject to the security measures below.

#### 5.2 Security Measures

* **Encryption:** Data in transit is encrypted via **HTTPS**.
* **Access Control:** Only authorized personnel can access non-on-chain data.
* **Reverse Proxy:** Used to prevent third-party tools from reading user data.

> **Reminder:** SwapX does not store your private keys or control your assets. You must safeguard your own wallet security.
